Potatoes O’Brien Ham, Bacon, Egg, Sausage & Cheese Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 bag (28-32 oz) frozen Potatoes O’Brien (with onions & peppers), thawed
  • 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 lb breakfast sausage
  • 1 cup diced cooked ham
  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk (or half & half for creamier texture)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (or cheese blend)
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack or mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9×13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate, leaving about 1 tbsp bacon drippings in the skillet.
  3. Cook sausage in the same skillet until browned and crumbled. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble the casserole:
    • Spread thawed Potatoes O’Brien evenly in the prepared baking dish.
    • Top with cooked sausage, bacon, and diced ham.
    • Sprinkle 1 ½ cups cheddar cheese and ½ cup Monterey Jack cheese over the meats.
    • Pour egg mixture evenly over everything.
    • Sprinkle remaining cheeses on top.
  6. Bake uncovered for 45-55 minutes, or until eggs are set in the center and top is golden brown.
  7. Cool slightly before slicing. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.

Notes

  • You can add veggies like spinach, mushrooms, or tomatoes for extra flavor.
  • For a spicier version, use pepper jack cheese or add a pinch of crushed red pepper.
  • This dish reheats beautifully and makes great leftovers.
